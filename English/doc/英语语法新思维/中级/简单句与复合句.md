一个完整的句子都是由两部分组成：陈述对象+陈述内容。

- 陈述对象（主语）
  - 名词
  - 相当于名词
- 陈述内容（谓语）表明主语怎么样了
  - 动词

# 句型二：主语 + 谓语

后面的谓语动词应该是不及物动词（intransitive verb)，所表示的动作没有作用对象，其本身意思完整，其后不需带宾语。

1. This trend began during the Second World War.
2. This trend began during the Second World War, when several governments came to this conclusion.
3. This trend began during the Second World War, when several governments came to this conclusion that the specific demands cannot generally be foreseen in detail.
4.  This trend began during the Second World War, when several governments came to this conclusion that the specific demands that a government wants to make of its scientific establishment cannot generally be foreseen in detail. 1996 年考研翻译真题。

# 句型三：主语 + 谓语 + 宾语

及物动词（transitive verb）

But the not-so-easy way may mean getting down on your hands and knees and pulling out the weeds by the root.

英语中大约 80% 是 “主谓宾” 结构。

- The emphasis helped *to obscure the great importance（不定式短语作宾语）*.（主谓宾）
- The emphasis *given by both scholars and statesmen（后置定语）* helped to obscure the great importance.（）
- The emphasis given by both scholars and statesmen *to the presumed disappearance of the American frontier（介词短语补充说明主语，表明强调的内容）* helped to obscure the great importance.
- The emphasis given by both scholars and statesmen to the presumed disappearance of the American frontier helped to obscure the great importance *of changes in the conditions and consequences of international trade.（介词短语作后置定语）*

学者和政治家们强调美国边疆小时的假设，这种强调模糊了国际贸易的条件和后果方面发生变化的巨大作用。

- The emphasis given by both scholars and statesmen to presumed disappearance of the American frontier helped to obscure the great importance of changes in the conditions and consequences of international trade *that occurred during the second half of the nineteenth century（定语从句作后置定语）*.

# 句型四：主语 + 谓语 + 间接宾语 + 直接宾语

该句型的谓语动词是**双宾动词**（dative verb）后面接人或者物。人表示动作的接受者，称作间接宾语（indirect object），物表示动作作用的对象，是动作的承受者，称作直接宾语（direct object）。

间接宾语和直接宾语合起来称为双宾语。

- He showed the guard his passport.

这种语句，一般可以改成把直接宾语提前置谓语后面，然后添加介词 to 或 for，再把间接宾语放在介词后面。用 for 还是 to 取决于谓语动词。

- He showed his passport to the guard.

to 

- assign, award, bring, deliver, deny, feed, give, grant, hand, lend, offer, owe, pass, pay, promise, post, read, recommend, sell, send, show, take, write...

for

- book(预定), buy, build, change, choose, cook, fetch, find, get, keep, make, order, prepare, sing...

有个别动词只能用这种句式，不能转，例如 charge, cost, bet

- I'll bet you ten dollars.
- The repairman charged me ten dollars.

还有特殊的， of 是所属格的意思，在初级中就已经讲过了。

- Can I ask a question of you?
- I have a question to ask of you.

难一点的

- <u>Her work in genetics(主语)</u> <u>won（谓语）</u> <u>United States scientist Barbara McClintock（间接宾语）</u> <u>the Nobel Prize（直接宾语）</u> <u>in 1983（时间状语）</u>.

# 句型五：主语 + 谓语 + 宾语 + 宾语补足语

factitive verb 宾补动词，宾语补足语（object complement）。宾语和宾语补足语喝起来称为复合宾语。

常见的带复合宾语的动词有：appoint，believe，call，choose，consider，declare，elect，feel，find，keep，leave，let，make，name，nominate，prove，see，suppose，vote 等等。
